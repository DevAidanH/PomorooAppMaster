What is Pomoroo?

Pomoroo is a fun, easy, and customizable Pomodoro timer to help you finish your work. Pomoroo is perfect for activities such as coding, writing, reading, graphic design, and so much more. This application utilizes the Pomodoro Technique, a time management method created by Francesco Cirillo.

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s. This productivity hack breaks up tasks into 25-minute intervals with a 5-minute break. Each interval is known as a Pomodoro. After four Pomodoros, users are typically encouraged to take a more extended break between 20-30 minutes.

How to Use Pomoroo

• Write your tasks for the day into the task box – add as much as you want
• Adjust the number of Pomodoros for each task
• Choose the task you want to work on
• Start the timer! Have fun and focus on the task at hand
• Repeat 3-5 times or however necessary until you finish the task
